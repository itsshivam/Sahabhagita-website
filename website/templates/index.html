    Our Motive

    Do you feel nostalgic whenever you pass by some school building? Do you recall the Saraswati Vandana, the melodious – “Ya Kundendu Tushaar Haar Dhavalaa” when you get a chance to hear the prayer of some school? Or the “Sahanabhavatu Sahanobhunaktu” with biting stomach sitting in the verandah during madhyavakash? Were you ever afraid of being called “Bhaiyya” or “Bahin” by your crush in school as that used to be the usual way of addressing each other? Does your heart beats fast recalling the numerous “Maasik Geets” you would have sung in chorus during Vandana? Or the notorious “Acharyaji” who made you do “Shaaririk” with peculiar jargon of “Daksha” and “Ahrama” alien to the rest of world? Do you, sometimes, miss those celebrations in the form of Jayantis, Kishor Sabhas, Jhankis, and other cultural activities?

    If the answer to most of the above questions is yes, it means that a true Vidya Mandirian is still alive in you.

    Friends, through this initiative we want to reach all the alumni of Saraswati Vidya Mandir so that we can bring them under one umbrella which we have named as ‘Sahabhagita’: a collective participation of Alumni.

    Through this platform we shall try to give back to our schools which helped us build our character and strong values that we adhere to.

    All the alumni of schools affiliated to Vidya Bharati are requested to please register to Sahabhagita. We are here to connect you with your alma mater.

    About us

    Sahabhagita is a collective endeavor to connect the alumni of schools spread across India affiliated to Vidya Bharati. It provides a proper forum to facilitate all round development of the nation.

    It acts as a link among the alumni and between the alumni and their alma mater.

    Sahabhagita is not just an organisation, it is actually an idea: an idea to connect with each other, an idea to participate collectively, an idea to bring positive change in the lives of many including ourselves, an idea to create a common platform where fresh ideas can blossom, an idea to give wings to our thoughts, an idea to bring the glory back to the nation called Bharat, which itself means “indulged in seeking knowledge”, an idea to... , this list can be endless.
